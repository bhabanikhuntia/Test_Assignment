Sahara

The word “Sahara” is derived from the Arabic word meaning “desert.”
The desert is known in Arabic as Al-Sahra Al Kubra, which translates to “The Great Desert.”

Western Desert

The Western Desert is mostly rocky desert, though an area of sandy desert, known as the Great Sand Sea, lies to the west against the Libyan border.

AI Bagawat

Al-Bagawat is an early Christian necropolis of the ancient town of Hibis (modern Khargah).

Pyramid of Giza

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the largest Egyptian pyramid and the tomb of Fourth Dynasty pharaoh Khufu.

Kalahari Desert

The Kalahari has existed as an inland desert since the Cretaceous period.
